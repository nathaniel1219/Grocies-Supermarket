About Grocies Supermarket

Welcome to Groceries Supermarket!
Where we pride ourselves on offering the freshest groceries sourced from local farms and trusted suppliers. Our commitment to quality extends to our convenient services, including delivery, catering, and party planning. Experience freshness and convenience at Groceries Supermarket today!



Our Branches

Mattakuliya Branch

Welcome to Grocies Supermarket in Mattakuliya! Our branch is uniquely positioned to provide the freshest products, thanks to our very own nearby Mattakuliya farm. This farm-to-table approach ensures that our fruits, vegetables, and other fresh items are harvested daily and delivered directly to our store, guaranteeing top quality and freshness. In addition to our farm-fresh produce, we offer a wide range of groceries, dairy products, meats, bakery items, and household essentials. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist, making your shopping experience convenient and enjoyable. Visit Grocies Supermarket in Mattakuliya today to enjoy unparalleled freshness and quality straight from our local farm.
