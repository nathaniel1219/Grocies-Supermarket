VEGETABLES

At Grocies Supermarket, our vibrant vegetable section offers a diverse range of produce sourced from three distinct farm branches. Locally, Mattakuliya Farm provides seasonal delights, while Batman Turkey Farm imports exotic vegetables. For a touch of Mexican flair, El Pito Mexico Farm supplies avocados and jalapeños. Explore our aisle and experience the flavors of Mattakuliya, Batman Turkey, and El Pito Mexico—all conveniently in one place!

Mattakuliya Branch

Our supermarket's farm in Mattakuliya offers fresh, high-quality vegetables like tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots, lettuce, and peppers. We use sustainable, organic farming practices, emphasizing water conservation and crop diversity. By supporting local jobs and promoting agricultural education, we contribute to the community. From our fields to your table, enjoy the best in fresh produce from our Mattakuliya farm.

Kirkwall Branch

Explore the bounty of Grocies Supermarket's farm in Kirkwall, where we cultivate an abundance of fresh, high-quality vegetables like tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots, lettuce, and peppers. Embracing sustainable, organic farming practices, we prioritize water conservation and crop diversity to ensure the health of our land and the purity of our harvests. By supporting local jobs and promoting agricultural education, we contribute to the community. From our fields to your table, enjoy the best in fresh produce from our Kirkwall farm.

El Pito Branch

Our supermarket's farm in El Pito is committed to delivering fresh, high-quality produce using sustainable practices. Nestled in the fertile region of El Pito, our farm grows a variety of vegetables such as tomatoes, lettuce, carrots, peppers, and cucumbers. We prioritize organic farming, water conservation, and crop diversity to ensure natural and healthy produce. Additionally, our farm supports the local community by providing jobs and promoting agricultural education. From our fields to your table, enjoy the best in fresh produce from our El Pito farm.
