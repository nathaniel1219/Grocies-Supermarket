DAIRY PRODUCTS

Our dairy aisle celebrates freshness and quality. At Grocies, we prioritize locally sourced ingredients. Our dairy journey begins with cows from the Mattakuliya Farm, nestled right here in our community. These contented cows graze on lush grass, producing milk rich in flavor and nutrients. We’ve also infused a touch of New Zealand magic—New Zealand cattle contribute to our dairy selection, yielding abundant and wholesome milk. From creamy butter to velvety yogurt, Grocies Supermarket brings you the best—from local pastures to distant New Zealand meadows.
